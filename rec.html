Welcome to The REC Army or The Reserved Events Corps

This is the "REC" page for Creative Universal, The REC Army was created by CU as a form of ROTC but for teens 13-19.

The REC Army is a group of teens that are trained to be the next generation of leaders, creators, and innovators. The REC Army is a place where teens can learn new skills, make new friends, and have fun plus learn some fundimentals of the real navy and dress like the army but our uniforms will be tailored to look different from the real deal to avoid any legal arguments with the governments.
